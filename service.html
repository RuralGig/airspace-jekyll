Coverage Map

The following map depicts the general area that we will be providing service for. However, service availability will vary by visibility to our tower at the center. Locations with a clear line of sight to the tower will be able to receive service even if they reside outside the circles. The farther out your location is, the less likely service will be available. We will be focusing on residents within the these coverage circles first and then expand outward.

Over time we will be growing and expanding with more locations and more towers. Our goal is to provide service to as many people as possible.

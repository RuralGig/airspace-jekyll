About Us

We are a Wireless Internet Service Provider (WISP), serving the Casco and Ganges Townships.

We will get you online with reliable high speed internet, where other providers have left you stranded. Using the latest in wireless networking technologies, provided by Ubiquiti Networks, bringing the internet standards that you expect to your home.

A small antenna, outside your house, will connect you to our main tower. Inside, it connects to your wireless router or other equipment, providing access to all of you devices. If desired, we can even supply wireless services for inside your home.

We believe high speed internet is a right

Just like you, we were frustrated in the available options to us in our great little community. The cable companies have little interest in getting you online. DSL might be available, but only to a select few locations, and it's slow and unreliable. Satellite might be fast, but the monthly limits make it unusable for any real internet activity.

We started this project trying to find solutions to this problem, and realized that these providers are just not willing to help us, so we are going to have to do it ourselves.

Our Services

RuralGig will work with you to get the service you need, at a reasonable price.

Connectivity

We try to provide a minimum of 15Mb speeds to each location. We will work with you if you desire faster speeds.

Unlimited Usage

There are no data usage limits or overage charges. You can browse and stream movies all month long.

Simple Rates

Upfront pricing and billing with no introductory fees or surprise charges.

Installation

We have certified technicians available to install the equipment at your premise.
